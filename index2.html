Our Journey

Jestel Nig. Ltd. was incorporated in year 2000 with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under the company and Allied Matters Act of the Federal Republic of Nigeria for carrying out services such as sales, supply and instalation of high quality security and safety equipments from leading Manufacturing Companies across the continents.

Since its incorporation in 2000, Jestel Nig. Ltd. has grown into a respected provider of security, safety, and construction solutions, serving homes, businesses, and institutions with world-class products and professional installation services.

Jestel deals in a wide range of security products in order to increase the safety and security of its clients in every way. We also sell and supply building materials and carry out construction works.

Our Values & Philosophy

Our design philosophy is built on four core principles that guide every product we sell or project we undertake.

Client-Centric Design

Every product provided is made with our clients' needs, preferences, and lifestyle in mind. Your story is at the heart of our process.

Sustainable Innovation

We believe in creating spaces that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible, using sustainable materials and practices.

Attention to Detail

From the grandest architectural elements to the smallest decorative touches, our products ensure every detail contributes to the overall vision.

Collaborative Spirit

We foster strong partnerships with clients, architects, craftsmen, and artists to bring the finest expertise to each customer.
